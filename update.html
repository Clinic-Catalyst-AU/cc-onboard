Update Your Claude

Two minutes. Copy, paste, done. Your Business Brain and your work are never touched - this only refreshes your skills.

The rhythm: click Copy → click into the black Terminal window → paste with Command + V → press Enter. That's it, every time. You never type the commands yourself.

1 Open the Terminal

Hold Command and tap Spacebar. Type Terminal. Press Enter.
A black (or white) window opens - that's where everything goes.

2 Paste the update, press Enter

Hit Copy below, click into the Terminal, paste with Command + V, press Enter.
curl -fsSL https://clinic-catalyst-au.github.io/cc-onboard/clinic-update.sh | bash
It runs for a moment and prints some text. When it says DONE, you're updated. If it ever says it couldn't download, check your wifi and paste it again.

3 Open a fresh Claude

Fully quit Claude Code and open it again - that's the important bit. New skills only show up in a brand-new Claude.
In the Terminal you can just type claude and press Enter to start it fresh.

That's you updated

Your Claude now has the latest Clinic Catalyst skills. Nothing of yours was changed - just the toolkit.

On a Windows PC instead? Use this in PowerShell:
irm https://clinic-catalyst-au.github.io/cc-onboard/clinic-update.ps1 | iex
