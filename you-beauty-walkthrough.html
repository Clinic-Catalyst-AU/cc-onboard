Past clients who haven't visited in a while get a gentle, honest reactivation sequence - a warm check-in in Amanda's voice, not a sales blast. Whoever books drops straight out of the sequence (the booked tag stops everything). Whoever doesn't rolls quietly onto the newsletter so they still hear from you.

How you run it: tag the contacts reactivation and the workflow does the rest.

The newsletter - set up once, refresh monthly

Your account holds 4 newsletter templates - one per week: Educate, Connect, Proof, Offer. The weekly workflow points at them once and never changes. Each month, one command refills all four with that month's editions in your voice:

/cc-newsletter-engine  -  writes the month's 4 editions, shows you each one for approval, then loads them into GHL for you.

One-time setup: watch the newsletter piece in your video library, build the weekly workflow exactly as shown, and point its 4 send steps at the 4 templates. After that, you never touch the workflow again.
