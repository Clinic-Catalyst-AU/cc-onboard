Facilitator Guide · Alicia's segment

The Lead Nurture Generator - your Day 1 block

2:45-4:00, the Meta lead-gen launch. This is the piece where every clinic walks out with a live campaign AND the instant follow-up already wired. Here is exactly how to drive it.

💡 What the tool actually is

The Lead Nurture Generator is a form on the toolkit. A clinic answers plain-language questions about THIS campaign's offer, and it writes their full 5-touch SMS + email instant follow-up sequence, AHPRA guardrails on, ready to paste into a GHL workflow. It is the "speed-to-lead" piece for the Meta campaign they just built. It is NOT the evergreen 7-email ladder - that is /cc-follow-up-agent, tomorrow (Day 2, your 9:30 nurture block).

The room flow · after the Meta lead form connects to GHL
1

Everyone opens the generator

Toolkit hub, Day 1 section, "Lead Nurture Generator". Click "See it with example data" on the big screen FIRST so they see where it lands - then clear it and make them write their own. Their words convert; Kelly's examples don't.

It saves per device. Answers auto-save on each laptop, so a refresh loses nothing. But it does mean they must fill it on the SAME laptop they'll present from.
2

Fill it in their own words - 15 minutes

Sections: the offer, the consult, proof + journey. Rough is fine, jargon is the enemy. Walk the room; the question you'll answer ten times is "what do I put for concerns" - answer: the words their patients actually say on the phone.

AHPRA on your shoulder: may help with, never guarantees; "special interest in", never "specialist". The generator builds with the guardrail on, but eyeball each clinic's output before it goes anywhere near live.
3

Generate, then paste into the GHL workflow

The output is the 5 touches in order. In GHL: Automation → the workflow shell you built in the Meta block (trigger = Facebook Lead Form Submitted) → paste each touch as its own step: SMS first (instant), email minutes later, then the timed follow-ups per the sequence.

4

Test it end to end

FB Lead Form Test Tool (button on the toolkit page) → submit a test lead → watch the contact land in GHL and enter the workflow. The test email should hit the clinic's inbox in the room.

SMS will NOT send yet - and that's correct. The sender-ID bundle was lodged Day 1 morning and takes 1-3 business days. Promise it exactly like this: "email is live now, SMS switches on the moment your registration clears". Never promise live SMS in the room.
5

Launch + the overnight review

Campaign submitted for Meta review before the group photo. It reviews overnight; you check approvals first thing Day 2 in your nurture block.

✅
Your segment is done when: every clinic has a submitted campaign, a workflow holding their own 5-touch sequence, and a test lead that received the first email. Anything short of that, note the clinic name and finish it with them Day 2 morning.
