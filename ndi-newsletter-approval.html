How this works

Your newsletter sends one email a week and then loops. These four are already loaded into it, so nothing needs rebuilding each month - when the month changes, only the words get swapped.

Each one has a job. Week 1 teaches, week 2 is you, week 3 is honest proof, and only week 4 asks anyone to book. That balance is deliberate, so your list never feels sold to.

Every button points at one booking link held in one place. When your offer changes, that link gets updated once and all four emails follow.
