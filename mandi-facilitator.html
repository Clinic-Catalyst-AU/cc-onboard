4 - Her big gap: get the patient list off the spreadsheet ~4 min

Mandi's medical-centre patient database is still in a spreadsheet, never imported into Timely. That's a warm list of people who already know and trust her as their GP - the fastest path to new cosmetic bookings.

  • Send her the Timely import guide (link above): consolidate the spreadsheet into Timely, then export for reactivation - Claude sections the list, import 50 at a time tagged reactivation, 2-3 days between batches.
  • The workflow fires per contact the moment each import lands - so batch size IS the send rate. Never bulk-import the whole list at once.
  • SMS steps stay OFF until her clinic number exists (same regulatory bundle as Aria).
