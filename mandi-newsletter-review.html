Pigmentation - one of our favourite things to help with

Welcome to a little note from our clinic. Each week we will share something useful about your skin, a little of who we are, and what we are up to in our new space - never any pressure, just here when you need us.

This week: pigmentation. It is one of the concerns we most love helping with, and one Dr Mandi has a special interest in. Those uneven patches, the freckling that crept in after a few too many summers, the marks left behind by old breakouts - they are incredibly common, and they respond beautifully to the right, considered plan.

Here is the part most people are not told: pigmentation has different layers to it, and what settles one type can stir up another. That is exactly why we never reach for a single machine. We look properly at your skin first, then tailor a medical-grade plan - often a blend of in-clinic treatments and the right at-home care - so we are always working with your skin, not against it.

If uneven tone is something you have quietly put up with, please know it is very treatable, and we would love to talk you through what is possible for you.

The concerns we love, and how we approach them

People often ask what we are best suited to. The honest answer is the skin we work with most: pigmentation, acne and the scarring it can leave behind, uneven texture, and softening or lost volume.

What ties them together is our approach. Every plan begins with a genuine, doctor-led look at your skin - your history, your goals, and what is realistic for you - before a single treatment is ever chosen. Medical-grade, TGA-approved, and built around you rather than around whichever device happens to be free that day.
