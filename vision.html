Every clinic walks out of two days with their own AI system running their marketing and their front desk. Here is the vision, the architecture, and the install - all in one place.

01 · The vision

We hand each clinic a system, not a service

Agencies rent clinics their marketing. We do the opposite: we install the capability inside the clinic, on their own Claude and their own GHL, and we leave it running. They own it. That is why it is worth so much more than what we charge.

One Business Brain teaches the whole system who the clinic is - their voice, offers, machines, the concerns they love, their team, their strategy. Then every skill reads that brain and writes on-brand, AHPRA-safe work automatically. Content, follow-ups, ads, the lot. The owner just glances and approves.

🧠

One brain, every output

Build the Business Brain once. Every post, email and ad inherits their exact voice - never generic, never robotic.

📧

Nobody left behind

The follow-up system catches every lead and walks them to a booking, then keeps past clients warm forever.

🛡

AHPRA-safe by design

Every line is screened against the advertising rules before it can reach a patient. Peace of mind baked in.

⏱

Their time back

Ten minutes a day of approving, not hours of doing. That is the promise - and the system keeps it.

02 · How it works

The architecture, in plain English

Every clinic gets one workspace folder. Inside it sits their Business Brain (the knowledge) and three output folders. The skills read the brain and write the work. Fixed names, fixed places - that is what makes sixteen skills behave as one machine.

~/Clinic/                        their workspace - everything runs from here
  Business-Brain/             the knowledge. skills READ here.
    brand-guide.md            voice + the master context (the spine)
    resonance-messaging.md    the emotional source-of-truth
    offers.md  services-machines.md  concerns.md  team.md  strategy.md
  Content/   Emails/   Ads/     the work. skills WRITE here.
  CLAUDE.md                   the rules + where everything lives

The build order is foundation-first. Nothing downstream runs until the brain exists:

Step 1

Resonance

You interview them (the emotional + offer work). Claude writes their resonance doc.

→
Step 2

Brand Guide

Claude turns it into the Business Brain spine - their voice, on paper.

→
Step 3

The system runs

Content, follow-up and ads all read the brain and produce on-brand work.

The bit that makes it sing: the foundation is not a form they fill. It is a warm interview - Claude asks, they just talk, and you do the emotional and offer work alongside. They watch their own brand get written for them. That is the moment the room lights up.
03 · The install

Clear as anything - two paths

There are two installs: yours (once, so you run the same system as Kelly) and theirs (each clinic, live in the room). Here is both.

A. Your install (once)

1
You

Get your accounts

A Claude account (claude.ai), and accept Kelly's invite to the Clinic-Catalyst-AU GitHub org.

2
You

Install the basics

Homebrew + the GitHub CLI, then sign in to GitHub.

brew install gh && gh auth login && gh auth setup-git
3
You

Run the bootstrap

One line installs Claude Code, Playwright, the CC skills, and your config.

curl -fsSL https://clinic-catalyst-au.github.io/cc-onboard/bootstrap.sh | bash
4
You + Kelly

Connect the keys

Paste the shared keys into your .env, connect the MCP connectors (Fireflies, Supabase, GitHub), and accept the shared Dropbox folder. Now you run the same AIOS as Kelly.

B. A clinic's install (live, in the room)

1
Before the day

They arrive with the basics

Claude account, an API key, a GHL trial + number claimed, and their business registration PDF from ABN Lookup. The pre-workshop checklist covers it.

2
Day 1 AM

Set up their machine

Install Claude Code and the CC skills together, screen by screen. Drive from one screen so the room follows.

3
Day 1 - the magic

Build their Business Brain

Run the resonance interview. You lead the emotional and offer work; Claude writes their resonance doc and brand guide into Business-Brain/. This is the foundation everything else stands on.

4
Day 1 PM

Phone, SMS + voice

Submit the regulatory bundle together (their ABN PDF), buy the GHL number, switch on the AI agents across every channel.

5
Day 2

Push the GHL snapshot + go live

Install the nurture snapshot, repopulate the templates in their voice, wire the workflows. Then the fun part - take it for a test drive and watch it all work.

The sweetener (if there is time): GitHub + Supabase + Render so they can build their own landing pages in Claude Code and copy them into GHL. Worst case, demo it and tell them it is already in their system - they can do it too.
